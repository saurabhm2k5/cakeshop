Our Story

Baking Since 2010

Le Prestige Cake Co. started as a small family kitchen with a big dream: to serve the most elegant and delicious cakes in the city. Our founder, Chef Neha, believes that a cake should look as good as it tastes.

Our Philosophy

We use only organic flour, farm-fresh eggs, and premium Belgian chocolate. We never use preservatives. Every cake is baked fresh on the day of delivery to ensure the "Prestige" standard.
